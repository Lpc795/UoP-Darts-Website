University of Portsmouth Darts Society 25/26

Welcome to the University of Portsmouth Darts Society! Whether you’ve been hitting 180s for years or you’ve never picked up a dart before, our society is all about fun, inclusivity, and building a community of players who love the game.

Who We Are

We’re a new society for the 2025/26 year, bringing together students of all abilities to enjoy darts in a relaxed but competitive environment. With regular training, inter-university games, and plenty of socials, there’s something for everyone – from complete beginners to aspiring pros.

What's Included

For just £25 for the year, you’ll get full access to:

  • Weekly Training – Every Monday evening from 6pm at Dockyard , 13–19 Guildhall Walk, Portsmouth PO1 2RY. Perfect for improving your throw, picking up tips, or just winding down with friends.
  • Competitive Inter-Uni Games (UDUK) – Every Wednesday, home and away fixtures against other universities! Home games will be held at our Dockyard venue, while away venues will be confirmed throughout the season.
  • Weekly Socials – From pub nights to themed events, there’s always something going on off the board too!

Why Join?

  • Be Part of Something New – Help shape a new society from the ground up.
  • Improve Your Skills – Regular training and competitive matches give you the chance to grow and develop your game.
  • Inclusive & Welcoming – No experience needed – everyone’s encouraged to join in and get involved.
  • Community & Fun – More than just darts – it’s about friendships, socials, and being part of a supportive team.
  • Rep the Best Society in Portsmouth – Match and training shirts will be available so you can play and train in style while representing the Darts Society wherever you go!

Get Involved

If you’re ready to be part of the University of Portsmouth Darts Society, come see us at Freshers Fair or drop us a message on social media. Whether you want to compete in inter-uni matches or just enjoy weekly socials, this is your chance to join a welcoming community and make the most of your uni experience.
